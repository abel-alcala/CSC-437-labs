Classic Spaghetti

Cooking Time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Serving Size: 4 servings

Difficulty: Intermediate

Description: A traditional Italian pasta dish featuring a rich meat sauce served over spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • Tomato
  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic
  • Ground Beef
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil

Preparation Steps

  1. In a large skillet, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat. Add 1 finely chopped onion and 3 minced garlic cloves. Sauté for about 5 minutes, or until the onions are translucent and fragrant.
  2. Add 1 pound of ground beef to the skillet. Break it apart with a wooden spoon and cook until fully browned, about 8–10 minutes. Drain any excess fat if necessary.
  3. Stir in 2 cups of tomato sauce (or crushed tomatoes), along with salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning to taste. Reduce heat to low and let the sauce simmer uncovered for 45 minutes to allow flavors to develop, stirring occasionally.
  4. While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add 400g (14 oz) of spaghetti and cook until al dente, about 8–10 minutes. Drain the pasta and optionally toss with a drizzle of olive oil to prevent sticking.
  5. Plate the spaghetti and ladle the meat sauce generously over the top. Garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese and chopped basil if desired. Serve hot.
